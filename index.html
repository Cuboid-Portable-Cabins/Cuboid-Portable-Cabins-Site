Products

PRODUCT

MS Pre-Fabricated Portable Office Cabin

MS Prefabricated Office Cabins are a cost-effective and sustainable solution for creating a functional office space in a short amount of time. Their high-quality material and construction ensure durability and longevity, making them a reliable investment for businesses looking for a flexible and practical office solution.

PRODUCT

MS Portable Premium Marketing Office

MS Prefabricated Premium Marketing office cabins are pre-engineered and prefabricated structures designed to provide a comfortable and convenient workspace for marketing and sales teams. These cabins are constructed using high-quality materials, such as MS (Mild Steel) sheets, and are built to last.

Product

MS Portable Farm House / Bunk House

MS Prefabricated Premium Farm House cabins are pre-manufactured structures that are designed to be used as a farm house or a vacation home. These cabins are made from high-quality materials. They are constructed off-site in a factory and then transported to the desired location where they can be assembled quickly and easily.

PRODUCT

Puff Panel Cabins

Puff panel cabins are prefabricated structures made from insulated panels that provide excellent thermal insulation and soundproofing. These cabins are constructed by joining together prefabricated panels made from materials such as polyurethane, polystyrene, or rockwool, which are sandwiched between two metal sheets. This construction technique makes puff panel cabins lightweight, durable, and easy to assemble. They are also customizable, allowing for a wide range of designs and configurations to suit different needs and preferences.

PRODUCT

Portable ACP/ MS Security Cabins

Portable security cabins can be customized to meet specific requirements, with options such as climate control, bullet-resistant materials, and surveillance equipment. They are a cost-effective and efficient solution for providing temporary or permanent security at various locations.

PRODUCT

Portable Washroom Cabins

MS portable washroom cabins are temporary structures that are designed to provide restroom facilities in areas where permanent bathrooms are not available or feasible. These cabins are constructed from mild steel and are often modular, allowing them to be easily transported and assembled on-site. They typically include features such as toilets, sinks, and lighting, and can be customized to meet specific needs and requirements. They are commonly used for outdoor events, construction sites, and remote work locations.

PRODUCT

Portable Coffe Shop Cabin

MS portable coffee shops or container shops are mobile and self-contained units designed to serve as small cafes or retail shops. They are often made from repurposed shipping containers, which are modified and fitted with equipment such as coffee machines, refrigerators, and display cases. They are an excellent option for entrepreneurs looking to start a small business without the high cost of renting or building a permanent location. They can be placed in high traffic areas such as parks, beaches, and street corners to attract customers.

PRODUCT

MS Portable Gazibo

MS portable gazebos are temporary outdoor structures designed to provide shade and shelter from the elements, such as sun and rain. They are typically made from lightweight materials such as aluminum or steel frames with a canopy or roof made of fabric. MS portable gazebos come in various sizes and designs, ranging from small pop-up models that can be set up quickly, to larger, more elaborate structures that require more assembly time. They are commonly used for outdoor events, such as weddings, parties, and camping trips.

Product

Studio Homes

MS portable studio homes container homes are innovative housing solutions that are constructed using repurposed shipping containers made from mild steel. These homes are designed to be affordable, sustainable, and portable, making them an excellent option for those seeking an alternative to traditional housing. They can be customized to include a variety of features, such as insulation, windows, doors, and plumbing, and can be designed to meet specific size and layout requirements. These homes are often used as backyard studios, guest houses, or even as full-time residences.
